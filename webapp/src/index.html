The sound of the ugly country

This is not Berlin, nor Paris or the Alps. Trends never make it here.

This is the sound of the ugly outdoors and small towns. Bleak lights and the thumping of an old diesel generator on a cold wet night. Slush, sleet and weeping winds over a forgotten bog. Move on! There is nothing here.

Bio

Growing old on Scandinavian Black Metal does things to you. It opens your ears, it seeps through the cracks and bleeds into the soul.

Pulsating noise and a love for the creative process brought us here. That, and solder fumes.
