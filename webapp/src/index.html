The sound of the ugly country

This is not Berlin, nor Paris or the Alps. Trends never make it here.

This is the sound of worn down commuter trains and small towns. Bleak lights and the coughing diesel of the suburb bus, on a cold wet night. Slush, sleet and weeping winds over rusty playgrounds.

Bio

The band biaspoint is a loose collective of creative individuals interested in exploring rhythm and noise using electronic means. It was born out of the ashes of the short-lived and now defunct solo project Gnostic Proboscis.

Post cold war body music, German kraut, Scandinavian metal and too much bunker techno. These are some of the ingredients that kicked of the inspiration and creative process of electro noise band biaspoint. Engineers using their skills to the detriment of humanity.

Pulsating noise and a love for the creative process brought us here. That, and solder fumes.
