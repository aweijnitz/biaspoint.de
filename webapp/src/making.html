Rolling your own gear

biaspoint is as much the process as it is about the results. We make our own creations for sound and visuals, not out of necessity, but out of our belief that the process reflects in the results. And the results will be truly unique to you, and reflected in you when you made the things yourself.

We do not build everything we play, but play most of the things we build. A good meal is carefully cooked with known ingredients, but if you strive to make it all from scratch, then you never eat!
Building is fun, but making music is fun too!
